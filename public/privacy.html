01 Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Tradevada, LLC ("Tradevada," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares personal information when you use our website, mobile or desktop applications, APIs, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service. This Policy is incorporated into our Terms of Service.

02 Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account information
Name, email, password (hashed), profile photo, time zone, and account preferences.
Trade data
Trade history imported from your connected brokerage(s) — including symbols, fills, prices, quantities, strategy tags, P&L, and your own notes, screenshots, and journal entries.
Brokerage credentials
Read-only API tokens or OAuth grants you provide to authorize Tradevada to import data from your broker. We never request your broker password unless explicitly required by an integration, and we never use credentials to place trades.
Payment information
Billing address, the last 4 digits of your card, card brand, and expiration date. We do not store your full card number — payments are processed by Stripe, Inc.
Usage data
IP address, device type, browser, operating system, pages visited, features used, referring URL, timestamps, and aggregate diagnostic information.
Cookies & identifiers
Small text files stored on your device for authentication, preference, and analytics purposes (see Section 8).
Communications
Messages you send to support, survey responses, and feedback you submit.

03 How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • provide, maintain, secure, and improve the Service;
  • create and authenticate your account;
  • import, process, and display your trade history and analytics;
  • process subscription payments and prevent fraud;
  • send transactional emails (account confirmations, billing receipts, security alerts);
  • respond to support requests and feedback;
  • send product updates and marketing emails (with opt-out available);
  • monitor performance, debug issues, and prevent abuse;
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your trade data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes, and we do not use your trade data to train third-party machine-learning models without your explicit consent.

04 Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract — to provide the Service you signed up for;
  • Legitimate interests — to operate, secure, and improve the Service;
  • Consent — for optional marketing communications and non-essential cookies (you can withdraw consent at any time);
  • Legal obligation — to comply with tax, audit, fraud-prevention, and other legal requirements.

05 How We Share Information

We share information only as described below:

  • Service providers (sub-processors). Vendors that help us operate the Service — including hosting (e.g., AWS or similar), payments (Stripe), email (e.g., Postmark, Customer.io), analytics (e.g., PostHog, Plausible), and customer support (e.g., Intercom). Each is bound by contract to use your data only to provide services to us.
  • Brokerage and data partners. When you connect a broker, the broker shares your trade data with us at your direction. We do not share your trades back with brokers or other data partners.
  • Legal & compliance. We may disclose information if required by law, valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Tradevada, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers. If Tradevada is acquired or merged, your information may transfer as part of that transaction. We will notify you and provide choices if your information becomes subject to a materially different privacy policy.
  • With your consent. Any other sharing will only happen with your explicit consent.

06 Broker Connections

When you connect a brokerage account, we receive trade and account data only as authorized by you and the broker. We use this data exclusively to populate your Tradevada journal and analytics. We request read-only access whenever the broker permits it, and we never use connections to place trades on your behalf.

You can disconnect a broker at any time from your account settings. After disconnection, we will stop importing new data, but data already imported will remain in your journal until you delete it or your account.

07 Payment Information

All payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. We never receive or store your full payment card number, CVC, or full bank account number. Stripe handles your payment data in accordance with its own privacy policy and PCI-DSS compliance program.

We retain limited billing information (last 4 digits, card brand, expiration, billing address) to display your payment method, send receipts, and prevent fraud.

08 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Strictly necessary — to authenticate your session, remember your preferences, and protect against CSRF attacks. These cannot be disabled.
  • Functional — to remember UI choices like your selected accent color, dark/light mode, or default account.
  • Analytics — to understand how the Service is used in aggregate. We use privacy-respecting analytics tools that do not build cross-site profiles.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. We honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal where applicable.

09 Data Retention

We keep your personal information for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Specifically:

  • Account & trade data — retained for the lifetime of your account; deleted within 30 days of account deletion (subject to legal hold).
  • Billing records — retained for 7 years for tax and accounting compliance.
  • Logs & security events — retained for up to 90 days.
  • Backups — encrypted backups may persist for up to 90 days after deletion.

10 Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you;
  • Correction — update inaccurate or incomplete information;
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and personal data;
  • Export — download your trade journal and account data in a machine-readable format (CSV / JSON);
  • Restriction & objection — restrict or object to certain processing activities;
  • Marketing opt-out — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in each email.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@tradevada.com. We will respond within the time required by applicable law (typically 30 days). We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

11 California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the rights described in Section 10, plus the right to know what categories of personal information we collect, sell, or share. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as defined under the CCPA/CPRA.

To exercise your rights, contact privacy@tradevada.com. You may also designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf, subject to identity verification.

12 EEA, UK & Swiss Residents

If you are in the EEA, the UK, or Switzerland, you have additional rights under the GDPR, UK GDPR, or FADP, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. We will cooperate with any investigation.

Our designated representative for GDPR matters can be contacted at dpo@tradevada.com.

13 Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your data, including:

  • encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256);
  • hashed and salted password storage (bcrypt or stronger);
  • network isolation, role-based access control, and audit logging;
  • quarterly access reviews and least-privilege provisioning;
  • regular security testing and vulnerability scanning;
  • incident-response procedures with timely user notification.

No system can be 100% secure. If we learn of a security breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and the appropriate authorities as required by law.

14 International Data Transfers

Tradevada is operated from Texas, United States. If you access the Service from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S. and other jurisdictions where our service providers operate. Where required, we use Standard Contractual Clauses or other lawful transfer mechanisms to protect your data.

15 Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact privacy@tradevada.com and we will delete it promptly.

16 Third-Party Links

The Service may contain links to third-party websites and services (such as broker login pages, news sources, or payment processors). This Privacy Policy does not cover those third parties' privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing them with information.
