01 Getting Started

Create your account

  1. Go to tradevada.com and click Get Started.
  2. Choose Monthly or Yearly — both unlock every feature and start instantly.
  3. Enter your email and create a password (we recommend 12+ characters).
  4. Complete checkout via Stripe — you get immediate access the moment payment confirms.
  5. Confirm your email and connect your broker via SnapTrade — trades begin auto-syncing right away.

Set up your first portfolio

From the dashboard, click Settings → Add Account. You can either connect a broker (recommended) or import a CSV. The dashboard, journal, calendars, and analytics all populate automatically once your trades are synced.

Customize your dashboard

Click the gear icon in the bottom-left to:

  • Choose an accent color (purple, teal, blue, amber, pink, or red).
  • Toggle Dark/Light theme.
  • Enable Monk Mode to hide dollar values for screenshots.
  • Reorder or hide tabs in the sidebar.
  • Configure which tickers scroll across the market banner.

02 Connecting Brokers

Supported brokers

Currently supported with direct OAuth sync:

  • Moomoo — read-only OAuth, near-instant trade sync.

CSV import is supported for any U.S. broker (Fidelity, Schwab, IBKR, Webull, Robinhood, etc.) via Settings → Import CSV. Direct integrations for these brokers are on our roadmap.

Connect Moomoo (3 minutes)

1

Open broker settings

From your dashboard, navigate to Settings → Connected Brokers.

2

Click "Connect Moomoo"

You'll be redirected to Moomoo's OAuth page. Tradevada requests read-only access — we cannot place trades, transfer funds, or change account settings.

3

Authorize

Sign in to Moomoo and approve the request. You'll be returned to Tradevada.

4

Initial sync

The first sync pulls your last 12 months of trade history (typically completes in under 30 seconds for ~1,000 trades). Subsequent syncs are real-time.

i
Privacy: Tradevada never sees your Moomoo password. The OAuth token is encrypted at rest and can be revoked at any time from your Moomoo account or from Tradevada's broker settings.

03 Analytics & Metrics

What each metric means

  • Net P&L — sum of realized profit minus realized losses. Doesn't include unrealized positions.
  • Win Rate — closed trades that finished above breakeven, expressed as a percentage. A 60% win rate is solid for swing trading.
  • Profit Factor — gross profit / gross loss. Above 1 means you make more than you lose. Above 2 means your wins are twice the size of losses.
  • Expectancy — average dollars made per trade across your full history. Multiply by trades / month for a forward expectation.
  • Sharpe Ratio — risk-adjusted return. Higher is better; 1.0+ is good, 2.0+ is excellent. Computed daily.
  • Max Drawdown — largest peak-to-trough decline in your equity curve.
  • Recovery Factor — net profit / max drawdown. How many drawdown-equivalents you've earned back.

Reading the equity curve

The equity curve plots cumulative P&L over time. Drawdown periods are shaded in light red. A smooth, rising curve with shallow drawdowns is the goal. Sudden cliffs usually indicate a single oversized loss — go investigate.

Calendar & heatmaps

The 12-month calendar heatmap colors each month by P&L (green = profit, red = loss, intensity = magnitude). The day-of-week × hour heatmap reveals when you actually make money — most retail traders are surprised to find their best hour is mid-morning, not the open.

04 Wheel Strategy View

Tradevada's wheel-strategy view tracks every cash-secured put, assignment, covered call, and called-away cycle for a single ticker. Premium accumulates against the underlying, and your "true breakeven" updates automatically.

Components

  • Step indicator — visualizes which phase of the wheel you're in (CSP / Assigned / CC / Called Away).
  • True Breakeven card — your effective break-even price after all premium collected.
  • Next-Move Simulator — pick a target monthly yield to model a matching strike, with projected premium and new breakeven.
  • Profit Projection Ladder — slide the price slider; see what you walk away with if called away at each strike.
  • Options trade history — every CSP and CC ever placed on this ticker, sortable.

05 Billing & Account

Plans & pricing

Two billing options, both with full access to every feature including SnapTrade broker auto-sync:

  • Monthly — $19/month. Billed every month, cancel anytime.
  • Yearly — $190/year ($15.83/month effective). Saves $38 a year vs monthly — the equivalent of 2 months free.

Subscriptions begin instantly at checkout. There is no free trial — you get immediate full access from the moment payment confirms.

Switching plans

Upgrade from Monthly → Yearly at any time from Settings → Billing. We pro-rate the upgrade. Downgrading from Yearly → Monthly takes effect at your next renewal.

Canceling

Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription. Your account remains active until the end of the current billing period. After that, your account converts to read-only — your trade history stays accessible for 30 days, then is purged unless you reactivate.

Deleting your account

From Settings → Account → Delete Account, you can permanently delete your account. We'll erase your trade data within 30 days (subject to legal retention requirements for billing records). You can also email privacy@tradevada.com for a manual deletion request.

!
Export before deleting. Once your account is deleted, your trade history is gone. Use Settings → Export → Download CSV to grab a copy first.

06 Troubleshooting

"My broker connection is showing 'disconnected'"

OAuth tokens expire periodically (typically every 30–90 days depending on broker). Go to Settings → Connected Brokers and click Reconnect. Trade history that synced before the disconnect remains intact.

"A trade is missing from my journal"

Click Settings → Force Re-sync to re-pull the last 90 days from your broker. If a specific trade is still missing after a re-sync, email support@tradevada.com with the broker's confirmation number — we'll investigate.

"P&L doesn't match my broker"

Tradevada uses FIFO accounting and calculates P&L based on individual fill prices. Some brokers use average cost basis or include fees differently. Settings → Accounting → Method lets you switch between FIFO and Average Cost.

"Can I change my email?"

Yes — Settings → Account → Email. We'll send a verification link to the new address.

Still stuck?

Email support@tradevada.com with a description of the problem and your account email. Average response time: 4 business hours during U.S. trading hours.
