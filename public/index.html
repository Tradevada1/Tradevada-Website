Playbooks

Find which wheel strategies actually print.

A playbook is a named options strategy — like "Conservative 30-delta wheel" or "Aggressive ATM CSPs." Tag each trade with a playbook in the Journal, and this tab shows which strategies collect the most premium, which tickers work best, and which to retire.

  • Strategy leaderboard — rank every playbook by premium collected, cycles, and win rate.
  • Tag from the Journal — attach trades to a playbook so performance accrues automatically.
  • Capital at risk — see what each strategy ties up against the premium it returns.
  • Retire the losers — find the wheels that work, archive the ones that don't.

Why we built it

The journal we always wanted.

It started on a small account in 2022. By trade #40 the spreadsheet was unrecognizable, the breakeven was wrong, and the broker's P&L said profitable when the account was barely breaking even. Premium collected on an assigned CSP changes your real entry; a rolled covered call shifts your breakeven again — and after a few cycles, no spreadsheet keeps up.

Every "trading journal" out there was built for day-traders flipping equities and shoehorned to fit options. So Brad and Hisham built the tool they actually wanted: cycle-aware accounting, true-breakeven projection, and a wheel view that tracks every premium ever collected against the underlying. Tradevada is the journal they wish they'd had on day one.
