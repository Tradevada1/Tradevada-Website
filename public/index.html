Wheel Strategy

Built around the wheel - not bolted on.

Tradevada treats your CSPs and CCs as one continuous cycle - every premium you collect reduces your true breakeven on the underlying, automatically.

  • Cycle-aware accounting - prior premiums cut your real cost basis, no spreadsheets.
  • Next-move simulator - pick a target yield, model a matching strike instantly.
  • Profit projection ladder - see total P&L if called away at any strike, breakeven highlighted.
  • Cycle tracker - follow every wheel from CSP through assignment to called-away on one timeline, with per-cycle premium and annualized return.
Calendar

Two calendars, one truth.

Scan your monthly P&L at a glance, then flip to the Contracts calendar to see every CSP and CC’s profit within each month - two views, one timeline.

  • P&L heatmap - color-coded by trading day.
  • Contracts calendar - every CSP & CC’s P&L by month.

Pack Watch

Every open leg watched. Every entry hunted.

Pack Watch keeps eyes on every position you hold and howls the moment one needs you. Live assignment-risk tracking, DTE windows, and custom alerts - plus "The Hunt" watchlist that scans tickers for the best cash-secured put near Δ 0.25.

  • Active Wheel Monitor - premium capture, DTE, Δ-assign, and AROC on every open CSP and CC, live from your broker.
  • Alert rules - profit target, assignment risk, DTE window, high IV, premium target, and earnings reminders.
  • The Hunt - add a ticker and get live price, IV, and the best CSP near Δ 0.25 in a 25-50 DTE window.
  • In-app & phone alerts - build a custom condition, get pinged the moment it's true.

Questions, answered.

Everything worth knowing before you start.

Is my brokerage data safe?

Yes. Tradevada connects through SnapTrade, a SOC 2-certified aggregation layer, over a strictly read-only connection - it can never place, move, or modify a trade on your behalf. You log in on your broker’s own page, so Tradevada never sees your broker password.

Do I have to pay to start?

No. The Free plan is free forever - manual trade entry and CSV Import from Any Broker are included, with no credit card required.

Which brokers can I auto-sync?

Robinhood, Webull, moomoo, and E*TRADE are live today, with more on the way. Any other broker works through CSV import. See the full list on the Supported Brokers page.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Pro and Wolf Pack Edition are billed monthly or yearly, and you can cancel whenever you like - you keep access through the end of your current billing period.

What exactly is the wheel strategy?

Selling cash-secured puts, getting assigned shares, then selling covered calls against them - repeating the cycle to collect premium. Tradevada tracks the full cycle and your true breakeven across every premium collected. New to it? Read the Wheel Strategy Guide.

Will you show ads or sell my data?

Never. Tradevada is a paid tool - no ads, no data selling.
