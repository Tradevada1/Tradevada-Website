Wheel Strategy

Built around the wheel — not bolted on.

Every other journal treats your CSPs and CCs like isolated trades. Tradevada treats them as one continuous cycle, accounting for every premium you've collected against your real breakeven on the underlying.

  • Cycle-aware accounting — premiums from prior CSPs and CCs reduce your true cost basis automatically.
  • Next-move simulator — pick a target yield to model a matching strike, with projected breakeven and contract count.
  • Profit projection ladder — see total P&L if called away at any strike, with breakeven highlighted.
  • True breakeven — updates in real time across infinite premium collections.

Pack Watch

Every open leg watched. Every entry hunted.

Pack Watch keeps eyes on every position you hold and howls the moment one needs you — then hunts for your next entry.

  • Active Wheel Monitor — premium capture, DTE, Δ-assign, and AROC on every open CSP and CC.
  • Alert rules — profit target, assignment risk, DTE window, high IV, premium target, and earnings reminders.
  • The Hunt — live price, IV, and the best CSP near Δ 0.25 in a 25–50 DTE window.
  • In-app & phone alerts — build a custom condition, get pinged the moment it's true.
