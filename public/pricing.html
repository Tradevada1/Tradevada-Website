Pricing questions

Is the Free plan really free forever?

Yes. Manual trade entry and CSV import are free with no card required and no expiration. You only pay if you want broker auto-sync and the Pro analytics suite.

What's the difference between Monthly and Yearly?

Same Pro features either way. Yearly bills once a year at an effective $15.83/month — about two months free vs monthly — and adds exportable trade history and a priority support SLA.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — Pro is one-click cancel from your account, anytime. Your data stays exportable.

What is SnapTrade?

SnapTrade is a secure broker-connection service that lets Tradevada auto-sync your trades read-only from your broker, so you never log a fill by hand again.

Is any of this financial advice?

No. Tradevada is a trading journal and analytics tool — not a broker, financial adviser, or signal service. You're solely responsible for your own trading decisions.
