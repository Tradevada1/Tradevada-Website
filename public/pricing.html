Frequently asked questions

Is the Free plan really free forever?

Yes. Manual Trade Entry and CSV import are free with no card required and no expiration. You only pay if you want broker auto-sync and the Pro analytics suite.

What's the difference between Monthly and Annual?

Same features either way, on Pro and Wolf Pack Edition. Annual bills once a year — Pro at an effective $16.67/month, Wolf Pack at $25/month — two months free versus paying monthly.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — Pro and Wolf Pack Edition are both one-click cancel from your account, anytime. Your data always stays exportable.

What is SnapTrade?

SnapTrade is a secure broker-connection service that lets Tradevada auto-sync your trades read-only from your broker, so you never log a fill by hand again.

How do payments work?

Payments are processed securely by Stripe. All major credit cards are accepted, and you can manage or cancel your subscription anytime from your account.

Is any of this financial advice?

No. Tradevada is a trading journal and analytics tool — not a broker, financial adviser, or signal service. You're solely responsible for your own trading decisions.
