Every release, fix, and improvement we ship — in reverse chronological order. Subscribe via RSS or follow us on X for live updates.

v3.0 May 3, 2026 MAJOR

The wheel update

A complete rebuild of the wheel-strategy view, plus a much faster journal and brand-new analytics. This is the biggest release we've ever shipped.

NEW

Wheel-strategy tracker. Cycle-aware accounting for CSP→Assigned→CC→Called Away. Tracks every premium ever collected against your true breakeven on the underlying.

NEW

Next-move simulator. Pick a target yield (% / month), get a suggested strike with projected new breakeven and contract count.

NEW

Profit projection ladder. Drag the price slider to see total P&L if called away at any strike. Breakeven highlighted.

NEW

Per-symbol performance breakdown. Win rate, profit factor, best/worst trade per ticker.

IMPROVED

3× faster journal. Pagination, search, and filtering now run client-side after first load.

IMPROVED

Light/dark theme switcher. Match your OS, plus a "Monk Mode" toggle that hides dollar values for screenshot sharing.

IMPROVED

Equity curve now shades drawdown periods.

FIXED

Calendar heatmap showed wrong colors for break-even days. Now renders neutral correctly.

v2.4 April 18, 2026 MINOR

News terminal & events calendar

NEW

News Terminal. Curated market headlines with severity flags (Low / Medium / High / Critical), filterable to tickers in your portfolio.

NEW

Events calendar. Earnings, dividends, FOMC, and economic releases — all in one timeline. "On Your Radar" widget surfaces events touching your open positions.

NEW

Configurable market banner — pick which tickers scroll across the top.

IMPROVED

Notification bell now shows broker-sync status and unresolved alerts.

FIXED

Tab reordering modal didn't persist between sessions. Now stored in your account.

v2.3 March 27, 2026 MINOR

Calendar trio

NEW

Three calendars in one place: P&L heatmap, options expirations grouped by date, and dividend ex-dates from your holdings.

NEW

Click any day in the P&L calendar to drill into the trades that closed that day.

IMPROVED

Options-calendar contracts color-coded by status: Expired/BTC, Assigned/ITM, Open.

CSV imports were getting old. v2 introduces direct read-only broker integration so your trades flow into Tradevada automatically.

Direct Moomoo integration. Read-only OAuth — connect once and trades sync within seconds of execution.

NEW

Multi-account support. Track personal, IRA, and margin accounts side-by-side.

NEW
